## Overview

This document details how the 203 matched CREEDS diseases were clustered using therapeutic area classifications from the Open Targets disease ontology. Therapeutic areas provide a standardized way to categorize diseases by the organ system or disease type they affect.

## Methodology

1. **Disease Matching**: CREEDS disease names were matched to Open Targets disease database using:
   - Direct name matching (155 diseases)
   - Synonym matching (48 diseases)

2. **Therapeutic Area Assignment**: Each matched disease inherits therapeutic area annotations from Open Targets. Diseases may belong to multiple therapeutic areas.

3. **Primary Classification**: For analysis purposes, diseases are assigned a "primary" therapeutic area based on the first listed area in Open Targets.
