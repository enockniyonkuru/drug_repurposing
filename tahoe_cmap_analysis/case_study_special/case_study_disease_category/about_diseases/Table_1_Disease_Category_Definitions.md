# Table 1: Disease Category Definitions Used in This Study

Disease signatures from CREEDS were classified into 20 therapeutic area categories based on Open Targets disease ontology annotations. Categories are ordered by the number of diseases in the primary analysis cohort (n=180 diseases with known drug associations).

| Therapeutic Area | N Diseases | % of Total | Definition |
|------------------|------------|------------|------------|
| Cancer/Tumor | 27 | 15.0% | Malignant and benign neoplasms, including solid tumors and hematological malignancies |
| Genetic/Congenital | 20 | 11.1% | Inherited disorders and developmental abnormalities present from birth |
| Nervous System | 18 | 10.0% | Neurological disorders affecting the central and peripheral nervous system |
| Immune System | 13 | 7.2% | Autoimmune conditions and immune-mediated inflammatory diseases |
| Gastrointestinal | 11 | 6.1% | Disorders of the digestive system including inflammatory bowel conditions |
| Musculoskeletal | 11 | 6.1% | Diseases affecting bone, muscle, cartilage, and connective tissue |
| Cardiovascular | 9 | 5.0% | Heart and vascular diseases including cardiomyopathies and vascular disorders |
| Infectious Disease | 9 | 5.0% | Bacterial, viral, fungal, and parasitic infections |
| Respiratory | 8 | 4.4% | Lung and airway diseases including asthma and pulmonary fibrosis |
| Hematologic | 7 | 3.9% | Blood disorders including anemias and coagulation abnormalities |
| Psychiatric | 7 | 3.9% | Mental health disorders including mood, anxiety, and psychotic conditions |
| Reproductive/Breast | 7 | 3.9% | Diseases of the reproductive system and breast tissue |
| Endocrine System | 6 | 3.3% | Hormonal disorders affecting endocrine glands and metabolism |
| Phenotype | 6 | 3.3% | General phenotypic conditions and syndrome complexes |
| Urinary System | 5 | 2.8% | Kidney and urinary tract disorders including nephropathies |
| Skin/Integumentary | 5 | 2.8% | Dermatological conditions affecting skin, hair, and nails |
| Metabolic | 5 | 2.8% | Metabolic disorders including lipid and glucose metabolism abnormalities |
| Pancreas | 3 | 1.7% | Pancreatic diseases including pancreatitis and exocrine disorders |
| Visual System | 2 | 1.1% | Eye disorders including retinopathies and ocular inflammatory conditions |
| Pregnancy/Perinatal | 1 | 0.6% | Pregnancy-related conditions and perinatal complications |
| **Total** | **180** | **100%** | |

**Notes:**
- Disease categories are derived from the Open Targets Platform disease ontology (Experimental Factor Ontology, EFO)
- Each disease was assigned to a single primary therapeutic area based on the first-listed annotation in Open Targets
- Diseases may have multiple therapeutic area annotations; primary classification was used for analysis
- 203 CREEDS diseases were matched to Open Targets; 180 had at least one known drug association and were included in the validation analysis

**Data Sources:**
- Disease signatures: CREEDS (Crowd-Extracted Expression of Differential Signatures; Wang et al., Nature Communications, 2016)
- Therapeutic area classifications: Open Targets Platform (https://platform.opentargets.org/)
