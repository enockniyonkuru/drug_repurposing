QC FILTERING CRITERIA APPLIED
=============================

QC1: Mean/Median Consistency
  - Requires: sign(mean_logfc) == sign(median_logfc)
  - Ensures: Directional agreement between mean and median logFC

QC2: Median Effect Size Threshold  
  - Requires: abs(median_logfc) >= 0.02
  - Ensures: Minimum meaningful effect size

QC3: P-value Filtering
  - Requires: adjusted p-value < 0.05 (where available)
  - Uses: adj.P.Val > p_val_adj > P.Value > p_val (in priority order)
  - Ensures: Statistical significance
