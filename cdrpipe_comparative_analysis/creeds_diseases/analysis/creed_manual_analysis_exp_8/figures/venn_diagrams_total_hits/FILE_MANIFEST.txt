INTERPRETATION GUIDE
================================================================================

VENN DIAGRAM REGIONS:

1. Left Circle Only (TAHOE Only)
   - Drugs identified by TAHOE but not CMAP
   - Indicates TAHOE-specific findings
   
2. Right Circle Only (CMAP Only)
   - Drugs identified by CMAP but not TAHOE
   - Indicates CMAP-specific findings

3. Overlap (Common)
   - Drugs identified by both platforms
   - Highest confidence candidates (validated by both)

CIRCLE SIZE:
   - Larger circles = more drug hits from that platform
   - Proportional scaling helps visualize platform differences

OVERLAP PERCENTAGE:
   - Calculated as: (Common / Union) × 100
   - Low overlap (< 5%): Platforms differ significantly
   - High overlap (> 20%): Platforms largely agree
