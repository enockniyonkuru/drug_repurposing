KEY FINDING 6: DISEASE-SPECIFIC METHOD PERFORMANCE
================================================================================

**CRITICAL OBSERVATION:**
This endometriosis study shows CMAP > TAHOE in hit volume, while the autoimmune 
study showed TAHOE > CMAP in known drug recovery. This suggests:

1. Different disease types may benefit from different computational approaches
2. CMAP may be more suitable for gynecological/hormonal conditions
3. TAHOE may be more suitable for immune-related conditions
4. Method selection should be disease-specific

**Manuscript Text:**
> "Interestingly, the relative performance of CMAP and TAHOE varies by disease 
> context. In endometriosis, CMAP identified 15.8-fold more candidates 
> than TAHOE, whereas in autoimmune diseases, TAHOE showed superior known drug 
> recovery rates (76.6% vs 17.8%). This disease-dependent performance suggests 
> that optimal method selection may require consideration of the underlying 
> disease biology."
